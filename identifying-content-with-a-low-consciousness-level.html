Identifying content with a low consciousness level

Method: Consciousness levels calibration (cal. 605)

Definition

Multiple different definitions seems to be reasonable.

Definition 1: Content which calibrates below consciousness level 200 on the Map of the Scale of Consciousness. The consciousness levels below 200 being regarded as “functionally false”.

Definition 2: Content which calibrates below a minimum level which is regarded appropriate for the type of content. E.g. science “should” mainly produce content which calibrates from 450 (inclusive) to 460 (inclusive).
